## What is TRK Blockchain?

TRK Blockchain is a **decentralized gaming platform** built on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC). Unlike traditional gaming websites:

- **No account creation** - Your crypto wallet IS your account
- **No passwords to remember** - Your wallet secures everything
- **Instant withdrawals** - Your funds are always under your control
- **100% transparent** - All games are recorded on the blockchain
- **Fair gameplay** - Results are generated by smart contracts, not by us

### How It Works

1. Connect your crypto wallet (like MetaMask)
2. Deposit USDT to play
3. Pick a number (1-10) and place your bet
4. If your number matches, you win **8X your bet!**
5. Withdraw your winnings anytime

## Setting Up MetaMask

### Step 1: Install MetaMask

1. Go to [metamask.io](https://metamask.io/download/)
2. Click **"Install MetaMask for Chrome"** (or your browser)
3. Add the extension to your browser
4. Click the MetaMask fox icon in your browser toolbar

### Step 2: Create Your Wallet

1. Click **"Create a new wallet"**
2. Create a strong password (you'll use this to unlock MetaMask)
3. **IMPORTANT:** Write down your **Secret Recovery Phrase** (12 words)
   - Store it somewhere safe (paper, not on your computer)
   - Never share it with anyone
   - If you lose it, you lose access to your funds forever
4. Confirm your recovery phrase by selecting the words in order
5. Your wallet is ready!

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **WARNING:** Never share your Secret Recovery Phrase with anyone. TRK Blockchain staff will NEVER ask for it.

## Adding BNB Smart Chain

MetaMask comes with Ethereum by default. You need to add the BNB Smart Chain network.

### Automatic Method (Recommended)

1. Open TRK Blockchain in your browser
2. Connect your wallet
3. When prompted to "Switch to BSC Network", click **Approve**
4. Done! MetaMask will add BSC automatically

### Manual Method

If the automatic method doesn't work:

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown (top of MetaMask, says "Ethereum Mainnet")
3. Click **"Add Network"**
4. Click **"Add a network manually"**
5. Enter these details:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Network Name | BNB Smart Chain |
| New RPC URL | `https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/` |
| Chain ID | `56` |
| Currency Symbol | `BNB` |
| Block Explorer URL | `https://bscscan.com/` |

6. Click **Save**
7. Select "BNB Smart Chain" from the network dropdown

## Making Your First Deposit

1. Go to **Wallet ‚Üí Deposit** in the menu
2. Enter the amount you want to deposit (minimum 10 USDT)
3. Click **"Approve USDT"** first

## Cashback Protection

Lost money playing? We've got you covered!

### How Cashback Works

1. Track your total losses over time
2. Once you reach **100 USDT in losses**, cashback activates
3. Claim **0.5% of your total losses daily**
4. Continue until you've recovered your losses

### Example

- Total losses: 1,000 USDT
- Daily cashback: 5 USDT (0.5% of 1,000)
- Days to recover: 200 days

To claim:
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Find the "Cashback Protection" section
3. Click **"Claim Daily"** (once per 24 hours)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this legal?
The legality of crypto gaming varies by country. Please check your local laws before participating.

### Can I lose my money?
Yes, like any game of chance, you can lose. Never play with money you can't afford to lose. The house edge exists but is transparent on the blockchain.

### Why do I need BNB?
BNB pays for blockchain transaction fees (gas). Each action costs a small amount (~$0.10-0.50).

### What if I forget my MetaMask password?
Use your Secret Recovery Phrase to restore your wallet. If you've lost your recovery phrase, your funds cannot be recovered.

### How do I know the games are fair?
All game results are generated on the blockchain using smart contracts. Anyone can verify the code on [BSCScan](https://bscscan.com).

### Can I play on mobile?
Yes! Use the MetaMask mobile app browser to access TRK Blockchain.

### What's the minimum deposit?
10 USDT to activate your account and play cash games.

### How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawals are instant! Once confirmed on the blockchain (10-30 seconds), USDT appears in your wallet.
