Frequently Asked Questions

What is TRK Game?
TRK Game is a blockchain-based prediction game platform on BSC Mainnet with a 15-level referral system. Players can earn through winnings and referral commissions.
How to start?
Connect your wallet (MetaMask/Trust Wallet) to BSC Mainnet, register with a TRK referral code, and receive your $100 practice bonus plus your own unique TRK code.
What is a TRK Code?
A TRK code is your unique referral identifier (e.g., TRK12345). It's easier to share than a wallet address and tracks your referral network automatically.
How much does it cost to play?
Registration is FREE with $100 practice bonus. To play cash games, deposit minimum $10 USDT. Practice games are always free (24 per day).
How do I win?
Predict a number (0-9). If it matches the winning number, you win 2X your bet as cashout plus 6X reinvested into your gaming balance.
How does the referral system work?
Share your TRK code. When someone registers with it, they become your Level 1 referral. Their referrals are Level 2, and so on up to 15 levels. You earn commissions from all levels.
What are the referral percentages?
Level 1: 5%, Level 2: 2%, Levels 3-5: 1% each, Levels 6-15: 0.5% each. Total: 15% distributed across your network.
How do withdrawals work?
Minimum: 5 USDT, Maximum: 5000 USDT/day. Withdrawals are instant via smart contract. For referral income, you need 100 USDT cumulative before first withdrawal.
Is my money safe?
Yes! All funds are managed by audited smart contracts on BSC. The platform is non-custodial - you control your wallet and funds at all times.
How many games can I play per day?
24 practice games + 24 cash games per day. Practice games use your practice balance, cash games use real USDT.
What wallets are supported?
MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and any WalletConnect-compatible wallet. Make sure you're connected to BSC Testnet (Chain ID: 97).
What happens if I lose?
Lost bets are distributed: 2% Creator, 5% BD wallets, 5% FEW wallet, 15% referral network, 65% game pool for future winners.
Can I play on mobile?
Yes! Use Trust Wallet or MetaMask mobile app. Connect to BSC Mainnet and access the platform through the in-app browser.
What is the practice game?
A risk-free game with an account with $100 bonus to learn the game. Practice winnings go to practice balance. Referral rewards from practice games are real USDT!
